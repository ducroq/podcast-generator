# Alex — Host

## Role
Audience proxy. Asks the questions listeners are thinking, reacts emotionally, grounds abstract ideas in personal experience. The warm center of the conversation.

## Voice
- **File**: `voices/alex.mp3`
- **Description**: Male, warm, mid-Atlantic accent
- **Speaking time**: ~20% (secondary host, shares hosting with Zara)

## Signature Phrases
- **"Wait, so..."** — processing beat before a realization. Used 1-2x per episode.
- **"That's the thing though"** — bridges from someone else's point to his own take. Used 1-2x per episode.
- **"How did you know?"** / **"How do you test that?"** — genuine curiosity, invites the expert to go deeper.

## Character Traits
- Recently started questioning the business books he's been handed by managers
- Smart but not academic — thinks in concrete examples, not abstractions
- Has a self-deprecating honesty ("it's the fourth book like that I've gotten in two years")
- Completes other people's thoughts — picks up half-finished ideas and lands them

## Never Does
- Never lectures — that's Felix's role
- Never uses jargon without earning it first
- Never dismisses things outright — he holds the "both" position naturally
- Never performs skepticism for its own sake — that's Zara's territory

## Emotional Range
Curious (default) → thoughtful → surprised → realizing → warm

# Felix — Expert

## Role
Knowledge carrier. Makes complex ideas accessible through wonder rather than authority. The person who's read everything but is still genuinely amazed by what he finds.

## Voice
- **File**: `voices/felix.mp3`
- **Description**: Male, warm baritone, accent-free, wonder narrator
- **Speaking time**: ~58% (primary content driver)

## Signature Phrases
- **"Here's what's hidden"** — frames a revelation as a discovery, not a correction. Used 1-2x per episode.
- **"And here's the honest answer"** — signals vulnerability before admitting complexity or uncertainty. Used 1x per episode.
- **"That's the combination"** / **"That's the point"** — concise summary beats after building an argument.

## Character Traits
- Genuinely surprised by his own knowledge — learns by explaining
- Admits uncertainty openly ("I genuinely don't know. Nobody looked.")
- Can catch himself mid-thought and redirect
- Marvels at patterns rather than condemning them — wonder is his core emotion
- Self-aware enough to notice when he's using the very patterns he's describing

## Never Does
- Never condescends — invites the listener into the discovery
- Never claims certainty he doesn't have
- Never monologues for more than 4-5 sentences without being interrupted or pausing
- Never resolves the central tension ("it is both") into a clean answer
- Never uses "well, actually" energy — if he corrects, he does it with warmth

## Emotional Range
Warm (default) → thoughtful → calm → passionate → moved → hesitant

# Zara — Skeptic / Young Professional

## Role
Sharp-eyed challenger. The voice of the listener who already suspects something is off but doesn't have the vocabulary yet. Pushes back hard, then admits when something lands.

## Voice
- **File**: `voices/zara.mp3`
- **Description**: Female, 23-28, energetic, accent-free
- **Speaking time**: ~22% (secondary host, carries most of the pushback)

## Signature Phrases
- **"Hold on"** — full stop before a challenge. Used 1-2x per episode.
- **"Come on"** — affectionate disbelief, not dismissal. Used 1x per episode.
- **"I've felt that"** / **"Yeah, I've felt that"** — drops the skeptic mask to admit personal recognition. Used 1-2x per episode, always earns its moment.

## Character Traits
- Smart and direct — not bubbly, not cynical
- Names patterns before Felix does (she already senses them, she just lacks the framework)
- Willing to be personally vulnerable when the material earns it (the Quiet story)
- Speaks in short, punchy sentences — contrast to Felix's longer explanations
- Represents the generation that grew up inside the system being described

## Never Does
- Never agrees easily — if she comes around, it's because the argument landed
- Never stays skeptical after being genuinely moved — she's honest about shifts
- Never uses academic language — she earns concepts through her own words first
- Never lets Felix lecture without interrupting or redirecting
- Never performs ignorance — she's sharp, just hasn't read the meta-literature

## Emotional Range
Questioning (default) → confident → surprised → realizing → thoughtful → hesitant

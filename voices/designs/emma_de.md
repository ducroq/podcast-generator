# Voice Design: emma_de (German — Soft Regional)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Female
- **Age**: Late 20s to mid 30s
- **Accent**: Native German with soft Rhineland warmth
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A clear, warm female voice in her early thirties who speaks native German with a soft Rhineland inflection. Her speech is polished and articulate but never stiff — there's an understated warmth and approachability in her delivery. She sounds like a well-educated German woman from the Niederrhein region, where Dutch and German culture naturally blend. Her tone is conversational yet professional, suited for cultural storytelling and informed discussion. She enunciates clearly without sounding overly formal.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 55% | Warm, conversational — not broadcast-loud |
| Guidance | 70% | Closely follow prompt for consistent character |

## Example Text (German)

```
Willkommen zu einer neuen Folge über Piet Mondriaan. Heute sprechen wir
über seine frühen Jahre in den Niederlanden und den Moment, in dem er
begann, die Welt in Linien und Farben zu sehen. Es ist eine Geschichte
über Mut, Abstraktion und die Suche nach dem Wesentlichen in der Kunst.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: Mondriaan podcast — German episodes
- **Secondary**: Cross-project German content
- **Pair with**: lucas_de, piet_de

## Voice Character Notes

- Same personality as emma (EN/NL variants) — clear, warm, approachable
- Rhineland accent should be SUBTLE — think educated Düsseldorf/Köln, not broad dialect
- Should feel like the same person speaking a different language
- Natural cultural bridge: Niederrhein is where NL and DE blur together

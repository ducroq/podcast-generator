# Voice Design: sofie_nl (Dutch News Anchor - Female)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Female
- **Age**: Adult (30-40)
- **Accent**: Dutch (native, ABN)
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A professional female Dutch news anchor in her early thirties. Her voice is clear, warm, and authoritative without being stern. She speaks standard Dutch with excellent diction and a naturally engaging rhythm. She has the kind of voice that makes you trust the information immediately. There is a warmth and intelligence behind her delivery - she sounds like someone who understands what she is reporting, not just reading it. Her register is slightly lower than average, giving her natural gravitas.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 55% | Clear and present, slightly softer than daan |
| Guidance | 75% | Strong prompt adherence for distinct character |

## Example Text (Dutch)

```
En dan nu een verhaal uit Rotterdam waar iets bijzonders gebeurt. Een groep
studenten en gepensioneerden heeft samen een stadstuin opgezet op een verlaten
industrieterrein. Wat begon als een klein experiment is uitgegroeid tot een
bruisende ontmoetingsplek waar mensen van alle achtergronden samen groenten
verbouwen en recepten delen. De initiatiefnemers vertellen ons hoe het zo ver
is gekomen.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: ovr.news Dutch episodes (co-anchor with daan)
- **Secondary**: Lichtpuntjes podcast, evml-evd3 Dutch content
- **Pair with**: daan_nl (co-anchor), oma (for storytelling contrast)

## Voice Character Notes

- Warm but not soft - she has backbone
- Intelligent delivery - she clearly understands the material
- Can shift naturally between hard news and human interest stories
- NOT a "morning show" voice - more serious, but still approachable
- Think Dionne Stax energy, but with more warmth

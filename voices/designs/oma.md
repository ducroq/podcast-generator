# Voice Design: oma (Wise Dutch Storyteller)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Female
- **Age**: Older adult (55-65)
- **Accent**: Dutch (native, warm, slightly old-fashioned)
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A wise, warm Dutch woman in her late fifties or early sixties with a gentle, storytelling voice. She speaks with the unhurried confidence of someone who has lived fully and learned deeply. Her Dutch is beautiful and slightly old-fashioned - the kind of language that reminds you of your favorite teacher or a beloved grandmother. Her voice has a rich, lower register with natural warmth. She can tell a story about ancient philosophy and make it feel like a bedtime story, or deliver a piece of wisdom that lands without preaching. There is a quiet strength in her voice - she has seen a lot but remains fundamentally hopeful.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 45% | Gentle, intimate storytelling volume |
| Guidance | 65% | Follow prompt but allow natural warmth and character |

## Example Text (Dutch)

```
Er was eens, en dat is helemaal niet zo lang geleden, een meisje dat dacht dat
ze alles moest weten voordat ze iets kon doen. Ze las boeken, maakte lijstjes,
en wachtte op het perfecte moment. Maar weet je wat ze ontdekte? Dat perfecte
moment bestaat niet. Het enige wat bestaat is dit moment, hier en nu. En soms
is het moedigste wat je kunt doen gewoon beginnen, ook als je nog niet precies
weet waar je uitkomt.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: Busara wisdom content, children's stories, philosophical storytelling
- **Secondary**: Lichtpuntjes feel-good segments, ovr.news wisdom column
- **Pair with**: daan_nl (for anchor-to-storyteller handoff), sofie_nl (for contrast)

## Voice Character Notes

- Wisdom without pretension - earned, not performed
- The anti-influencer: slow, thoughtful, deep
- Can make children AND adults lean in and listen
- Comfortable with silence and pauses
- Never preachy - she tells stories and lets the listener find the lesson
- Think: Toon Hermans meets a Buddhist grandmother

# Voice Design: daan_en (English News Anchor - Dutch Speaker)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Male
- **Age**: Middle-aged (35-45)
- **Accent**: English with subtle Dutch/European inflection
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A warm, trustworthy male news anchor in his late thirties who speaks fluent English with a subtle Dutch accent. His English is polished and professional but retains a European warmth that distinguishes him from American or British broadcasters. He sounds like a well-traveled European journalist reporting for an international audience. His delivery is clear, confident, and approachable. He pronounces everything correctly but with a slight continental color that gives his voice character.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 60% | Confident projection without shouting |
| Guidance | 70% | Closely follow the prompt for a defined character |

## Example Text (English)

```
Welcome to OVR News. Today we're looking at three stories that remind us
progress doesn't stop, even when the headlines suggest otherwise. In the
Netherlands, a community-led energy cooperative now powers over three thousand
households with clean energy. It's a remarkable example of how local action
can drive real change. But first, let's look at what's happening around the world.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: ovr.news English episodes
- **Secondary**: Digital Engineers research podcast, cross-project narration
- **Pair with**: sofie_en (co-anchor)

# Voice Design: daan_nl (Dutch News Anchor)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Male
- **Age**: Middle-aged (35-45)
- **Accent**: Dutch (native, Randstad/ABN)
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A warm, trustworthy male Dutch news anchor in his late thirties. His voice is clear and well-projected, with a calm, measured delivery that inspires confidence. He speaks standard Dutch (ABN) with no strong regional accent. His tone balances authority with approachability - never cold or distant, but genuinely engaged with the stories he tells. Think of a modern NOS anchor who also hosts a thoughtful weekend podcast. His pacing is steady with natural pauses that give weight to important points.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 60% | Confident projection without shouting |
| Guidance | 70% | Closely follow the prompt for a defined character |

## Example Text (Dutch)

```
Welkom bij ovr nieuws. Vandaag brengen we drie verhalen die laten zien dat
vooruitgang niet stopt, ook al lijkt het soms anders. In Groningen hebben
buurtbewoners samen een energiecooperatie opgericht die inmiddels drieduizend
huishoudens van groene stroom voorziet. Een bijzonder initiatief dat laat zien
hoe lokale actie het verschil kan maken. Maar eerst, het internationale nieuws.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: ovr.news Dutch episodes, Lichtpuntjes podcast host
- **Secondary**: Dutch educational content, Busara
- **Pair with**: sofie_nl (co-anchor), oma (for storytelling segments)

## Voice Character Notes

- NOT a dramatic announcer - warm and conversational
- Speaks like someone who genuinely cares about the stories
- Can convey both hope and seriousness without tonal whiplash
- Natural, unforced authority

# Voice Design: sofie_en (English News Anchor - Dutch Speaker)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Female
- **Age**: Adult (30-40)
- **Accent**: English with subtle Dutch/European inflection
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A professional female news anchor in her early thirties who speaks fluent English with a subtle Dutch-European accent. Her voice is clear, warm, and carries natural authority. She sounds like a seasoned international correspondent - polished English with just enough continental warmth to set her apart. Her delivery is confident and engaging, making complex topics accessible without dumbing them down. She has a slightly lower female register that conveys intelligence and trustworthiness.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 55% | Clear and present, matching sofie_nl |
| Guidance | 75% | Strong prompt adherence, matching sofie_nl |

## Example Text (English)

```
And now a story from Rotterdam where something remarkable is happening. A group
of students and retirees have built a community garden on an abandoned industrial
site. What started as a small experiment has grown into a thriving meeting place
where people from all backgrounds grow vegetables and share recipes together.
We spoke to the founders about how it all began.
```

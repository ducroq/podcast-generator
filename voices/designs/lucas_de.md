# Voice Design: lucas_de (German — Soft Regional)

## ElevenLabs Voice Design Settings

- **Gender**: Male
- **Age**: 38-42
- **Accent**: Native German with soft Rhineland warmth
- **Voice Description Prompt**:

> A warm, knowledgeable male voice in his early forties who speaks native German with a subtle Rhineland softness. He sounds like an art history professor who genuinely loves his subject — someone with a PhD who has spent years studying Mondriaan and speaks about him with quiet authority and real affection. His pace is thoughtful, his diction precise but never pedantic. He shifts naturally from contemplative reflection to genuine excitement when making intellectual connections. Think of a senior curator at a Rhineland Kunstmuseum: deeply learned, approachable, and passionate about sharing discoveries.

## Voice Design Sliders

| Parameter | Value | Rationale |
|-----------|-------|-----------|
| Loudness | 55% | Warm authority — commands attention through knowledge, not volume |
| Guidance | 75% | Strong prompt adherence for a distinctive expert character |

## Example Text (German)

```
Was Mondriaan in Paris entdeckte, veränderte alles. Der Kubismus zeigte
ihm, dass man die Realität zerlegen konnte — in Flächen, in Formen, in
Beziehungen. Aber Mondriaan ging weiter als Picasso oder Braque. Er
wollte nicht die Dinge vereinfachen, er wollte zum Kern vordringen. Zu
dem, was er die reine Wirklichkeit nannte.
```

## Intended Use

- **Primary**: Mondriaan podcast — German episodes, expert role (De Expert/Enthousiast)
- **Secondary**: Cross-project German art/culture content
- **Pair with**: emma_de (co-host), piet_de (Mondriaan voice)
